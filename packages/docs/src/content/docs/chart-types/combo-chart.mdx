# Combo Chart

Combo charts combine multiple chart types (line, bar, area) in a single visualization, optionally with dual Y-axes. Use them when you need to compare measures with different scales or show different data perspectives together.

### Dual Axis (`dualAxis`)

When enabled, a second Y-axis appears on the right side of the chart. Assign series to either the left or right axis based on their scale.

## Tips

- Use **dual axes** when measures have very different scales (e.g., revenue in millions vs. count in hundreds)
- Keep it simple — **2–3 series maximum** for readability
- Label each axis clearly so viewers understand which series maps to which scale
- The combo chart is the most flexible but also the easiest to over-complicate
