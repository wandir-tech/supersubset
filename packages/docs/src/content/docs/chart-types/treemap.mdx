# Treemap

Treemaps display hierarchical data as nested rectangles, where each rectangle's area is proportional to its value. Useful for showing composition and quickly identifying the largest categories.

<ExpandAll />

## Default Appearance

A treemap with default settings shows colored rectangles proportional to their values.

## Tips

- Best for **showing relative size** across many categories at once
- Works well with **2–3 levels** of hierarchy
- For part-to-whole with few categories, a [Pie Chart](/chart-types/pie-chart/) may be clearer
